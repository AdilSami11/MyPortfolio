Frontend-Development

Front-end development is the process of creating the user interface (UI) for a website or application. It involves using markup languages and other tools to design the elements that users interact with, such as buttons and text.

UI/UX Design

UX involves conducting user research, defining information architecture, creating wireframes, and usability testing. UI design focuses on the visual, interactive properties of the product. It involves designing the layout, functionality, and visual aspects (e.g. color, icons, typography) of each screen.

Video Editing

Video editing is the art of manipulating and combining video files to create a completed video project. Video Editors will cut together film clips, alter and correct sound mixing, add digital effects, and make other essential technical changes to video files.
